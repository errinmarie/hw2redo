Errin Mixon is a writer and travel specialist based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Currently Director of Global Sales for Geographic Expeditions, Errin's career has taken her around the world.

From the sprawling fortresses of India to the cliff-clinging monasteries in Bhutan; through the mazes of Egypt's bazaars and Vietnam's hidden caves; around the holy stupas of the Himalaya and the mountains of the Silk Road; across crowded European cities and vast African plains, Errin has wandered. She plans travel for sojourners and writes stories for dreamers.
