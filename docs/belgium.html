Belgium is...Boring

My husband and I both agree: Belgium is pretty boring. To be fair, we'd just come from Amsterdam, which had similar sights and a much cooler vibe. But after you've taken your pictures, there's not much to do in Bruges or Ghent, and the chocolate was not all that was promised.

On the upside, I got a lot of reading done.
