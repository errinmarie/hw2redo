India: Land of Spices

India is awesome, and remains one of the coolest places I've ever visited. I went with my brother, which was awkward, cause everyone assumed we were married and kept arranging romantic dinners and double beds for us.

I ate my way through that trip. I ate like it was my J.O.B. There was just so much food. Curries and naans and parathas and chutneys and rice, and oh God, the picked garlic! Did you know there was such a thing?? I put it on every dish and it was amazing. There were days we spent more time eating than touring. After one abundant lunch (and romantic elephant ride), I nearly wept when we were presented with a multi-course candlelit dinner, and a staff of four to serve us.

"I'm gonna puke," my brother whispered to me.

"Whatever," I said. "Just do it quietly and come back and eat this so we don't hurt their feelings."
