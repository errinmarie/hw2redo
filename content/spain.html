The Pain in Spain

I went to Spain with my college choir. It was years ago and I don't remember much about it, but I do recall that we went to see a bullfight, and for some reason I was astonished that they actually killed the bulls. It was hard to watch. And those things go on forever. We sat through 3 rounds of dead bulls, then decided we'd had enough and got up to leave. And the people started hissing and spitting at us! Apparently it is very rude to leave a bullfight in the middle. Ruder, it seems, than killing a bull.
